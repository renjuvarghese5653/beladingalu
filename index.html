AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF THE TRUST

  • To provide education and vocational training, consistent with national programs and effective utilization of trained persons.
  • To facilitate education, professional training to the students coming from rural background while pursuance of studies and by arranging courses for degrees, diplomas and certificate in technical training and to provide in-house training or arrange in-housing training in institutions.
  • To give grants, subsidies or other assistance by conducting camps, training program and establishing centers to provide the much-needed accommodation and support to students.
  • Organizing specific and subject oriented lectures, seminars and publication to spread information and knowledge benefiting artisans.
  • To undertake, carryout, initiate, support and/or promote social welfare and service activities for the relief of the poor, needy, indigent, under privileged, and/or working people, irrespective of religion, caste, creed, sect, class or community and to provide facilities and training to them in addressing and solving their personal, economic, medical, social and family difficulties and by way of distribution of text books, note books, clothes, uniforms and/or meals.
  • To initiate, support and/or promote education in cities, towns, villages and mofussil areas and to open, set-up administer, organize, manage, encourage, support, associate, supervise, grant, financial aid, run and / or operate kindergarten schools, pre-primary, primary, middle and higher educational schools, vocational schools, colleges, institutions and / or technical centers with a view to provide and impart school and collegiate education, courses in professional education, vocational training, non-formal education and / or job oriented education.
  • To support, set up, administer, organize, manage, encourage, associate, supervise, promote and carry out programmes conducted by the Government and / or by voluntary organization(s) and / or individual(s) for the benefit of the poor needy and downtrodden to provide for their basic needs of food, clothing, shelter, health, education, housing and employment; the economically and socially handicapped, the destitute and the weaker sections of society in relation to their education, health, social and economic welfare and promotion of their over all human dignity or their growth and development and / or by providing financial assistance;
  • To set up, maintain and / or administer homes for the aged, shelters, clinics, hostels, counseling centers, rehabilitation centers, etc., for the benefit of the old aged and senior citizens, to support, administer, organize, manage, encourage, associate, supervise, grant financial aid and / or carry out programmes for the benefit of the old and aged (those past the age of sixty and who have retired from gainful service or employment) and / or by providing financial assistance to them to meet the expenses of house rents, deposit for old age homes, expenses of daily living, nursing care, medical care and treatment, hospital charges, equipment like wheel chairs, walkers and walking sticks, etc., and the like concerned with their overall human well-being and / or by collaborating, co-operating and providing financial assistance to those engaged in providing such services.
  • To provide soft-loans (free of interest), bridge-loans, scholarships, grants, subsidies, etc., to any deserving individual or the ward of any individual in attainment of any of the aforesaid objectives towards meeting the expenses of his or her education, medical care employment (including self employment), marriage, agricultural development, rural development or for any other purpose which will help towards the attainment of any of the above objectives according to the schemes framed from time to time and / or to any other society or trust or institution involved in objects of similar nature.
  • To establish, promote or take over blood banks, eye bank, burn centers, mobile medical services, primary health care education and awareness to conduct medical, dental, eye check camps etc.
  • To conduct, promote, organize, plan and execute various projects for the improvement of the women and child development programmes to promote agriculture development, environmental programmes, social, animal welfare programmes and various rural welfare programmes.
